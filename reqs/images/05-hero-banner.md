# Image 5: Hero Banner

**Filename:** `hero.png`
**Aspect Ratio:** 21:9 (ultrawide banner)
**Resolution:** 2K

## Prompt

In the style of a modern wellness editorial illustration: clean, soft contour lines with minimal anatomical detail. Flat color fills with subtle warm shading. The color palette is muted and calming -- soft ivory skin tones, warm taupe and bronze shadows, accents of dusty sage and muted rose. The aesthetic is therapeutic and approachable, like a high-end wellness brand. Simplified, elegant human anatomy with smooth contours. No text, no labels, no watermarks.

A wide, panoramic wellness illustration. On the right side of the composition, a softly feminine figure sits cross-legged on the ground in a gentle, restorative stretch -- one arm reaching slowly overhead in a calm lateral side bend. The figure is rendered in the same warm, simplified style as the body map illustrations. The pose communicates ease, not effort.

The left two-thirds of the image is open space with a soft, warm gradient background that transitions from pale ivory on the left to a gentle dusty sage on the right where the figure sits. Subtle, organic flowing shapes in muted rose and warm taupe float gently in the background, evoking breath, movement, and the nervous system at rest. These shapes are abstract and decorative, like soft watercolor washes -- not literal objects.

The overall composition leaves generous negative space on the left for text overlay. The mood is quiet, restorative, and deeply calming -- like the first deep breath of the morning.

## Purpose
Main landing page hero banner. Text (site name, tagline) will be overlaid on the left portion programmatically. The figure on the right connects visually to the body map experience.
